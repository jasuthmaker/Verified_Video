## COMPREHENSIVE PRODUCTION SPECIFICATIONS

### **OVERALL TONE & STYLE**
- **Aesthetic**: Modern SaaS, minimalist, professional, trustworthy
- **Pacing**: Dynamic but not rushed. Scenes build narrative momentum.
- **Mood**: Confident, empathetic, solution-focused
- **Target Feeling**: "Finally, someone solved this problem the right way."

### **VOICEOVER SPECIFICATIONS**
- **Narrator**: Professional female voice (warm, empathetic, trustworthy)
- **Accent**: Clear, neutral American English or UK English (your choice)
- **Pace**: Conversational, ~140 words per minute
- **Tone Modulation**:
  - Scene 1-2: Empathetic concern (relatable problem)
  - Scene 3-5: Positive, solution-focused (confidence building)
  - Scene 6-8: Warm, engaging (student perspective)
  - Scene 9: Professional, analytical (teacher insights)
  - Scene 10: Serious, trustworthy (privacy assurance)
  - Scene 11: Triumphant, final CTA (call to action)

### **MUSIC & SOUND DESIGN**
- **Background Music**:
  - Scenes 1-2: Subtle tension (minor key, ~60 BPM, ~-20dB)
  - Scenes 3-5: Uplifting (major key, ~80 BPM, inspirational)
  - Scenes 6-8: Warm, educational (modern, minimalist, ~60 BPM)
  - Scene 9: Professional, analytical (tech-forward, ~90 BPM)
  - Scene 10: Trustworthy, serious (minimal, ~70 BPM)
  - Scene 11: Triumphant conclusion (major swell, ~100 BPM)

- **Sound Effects**:
  - UI interactions: Soft, musical tones (avoid harsh beeps)
  - Success feedback: Pleasant chimes (220Hz-330Hz range)
  - Warnings: Subtle alerts (not alarming)
  - Button clicks: Light, subtle (~50ms, ~1000Hz)
  - Transitions: Smooth, integrated with music

- **Audio Mixing**:
  - Voiceover: -0dB (reference level)
  - Music: -12dB to -15dB under voiceover
  - SFX: -18dB to -20dB (supporting elements)
  - Total audio: -0.3dB (slight headroom)

### **COLOR PALETTE (THROUGHOUT)**
- **Primary**: Modern Blue (#0066CC or similar)
- **Secondary**: Clean White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent**: Trust Green (#22C55E or similar)
- **Warning**: Amber/Yellow (#F59E0B)
- **Alert**: Muted Red (#EF4444, not aggressive)
- **Neutral**: Light Gray (#F3F4F6), Dark Gray (#374151)
- **Backgrounds**: Pure white or very light blue/gray gradients

### **TYPOGRAPHY**
- **Font Family**: Modern, sans-serif (Helvetica Neue, Inter, or similar)
- **Sizes**: Large, readable on all screens
- **Weights**: Mix of regular (400) and semi-bold (600) for hierarchy
- **Styles**: Professional, no script fonts or decorative typefaces

### **CAMERA & CINEMATOGRAPHY**
- **Shots**:
  - Wide shots (establishing context)
  - Medium shots (teacher/student perspective)
  - Close-ups (UI details, emotions)
  - Over-the-shoulder (watching activity)
  - Overhead (desk/computer work)
  - Split screen (comparison)

- **Movement**:
  - Slow push-ins (drawing attention)
  - Smooth pans (following action)
  - Gentle zooms (revealing details)
  - Static holds (for key moments)

- **Depth of Field**:
  - Shallow DOF for human subjects (teachers, students)
  - Slightly deeper for UI elements (maintaining clarity)

### **ANIMATION SPECIFICATIONS**
- **Principles**: Smooth, professional, avoid jarring transitions
- **Easing Functions**:
  - Ease-out (entering elements): Cubic, Quad
  - Ease-in (exiting elements): Cubic, Quad
  - Spring (celebratory moments): Elastic or Spring easing
  - Linear (progress bars, continuous elements)

- **Duration Standards**:
  - UI element appearances: 0.3s - 0.5s
  - Transitions between scenes: 0.5s - 1.0s
  - Progress bar fills: 1.5s - 2.5s
  - Chart animations: 0.1s stagger between elements
  - Celebration effects: 1.5s - 2.0s

- **Avoid**:
  - Overly long animations (boring)
  - Excessive animations (distracting)
  - Jarring cuts (use transitions instead)
  - Unnatural movements (respect physics)
